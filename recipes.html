The BEST (GF) Chocolate Chip Cookies!

Ingredients

  • 125 g butter
  • 125g brown sugar
  • 100g caster sugar
  • 400g GF flour
  • 200g chocolate
  • pinch of salt
  • 1 egg

Optional

  • espresso
  • dark chololate
  • browned butter
  • coconut flakes
  • toffee chips
  • flakey salt
  • nuts

Directions

Step One

Combine the sugar and butter in a bowl until mixed well.

Step Two

Add in one egg and mix until the mixture has changed from a dark brown to a lighter brown. (I reccomend mixing this manually with a whisk)

Step Three

Once this mixture is ready, sieve the flour and salt, then add half to the mixture. Combine with a spatula or wooden spoon.

Step Four

Add in the second half of the flour and mix until you have cookie dough.

Step Five

Add in the chocolate and any other ingredients you like, such as hazelnuts.

Step Six

Use an ice cream scoop to measure even amounts of dough, and roll in your hands until it has formed a ball.

Step Seven

Transfer to a parchment lined tray and place in a pre-heated oven at 180 degrees celsius for 10 minutes. After 10 minutes, turn the tray around and leave in the oven for another 2/3 minutes for an even bake.

Step Eight

Once out of the oven, allow cool for 10 minutes before transferring to a wire rack to cool further.
