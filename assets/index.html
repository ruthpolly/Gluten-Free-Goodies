Why Try Gluten Free Recipes?

What Is Gluten?

Gluten is a protein found in wheat, barley and rye. The glutelin and prolamin proteins store nutrients in plants, and have different names in each plant category. gluten in the umbrella term used for each of these proteins. Gluten is a helpful protein in baking as it acts like a glue, holding the dough together. Once bakes, it gives a soft, chewy texture.

What Is Coeliac Disease?

Coeliac Disease is a serious, genetic autoimmune disease caused by consuming gluten. When someone with this disease consumes gluten, it damages the villi in the small intestine. This affects the bodies ability to absorb nutrients from food. This can lead to malnutrition and other problems such as cancer, osteoporosis, thyroid disease and and infertility.

Why Make Meals/Desserts Gluten-Free?

While most people are able to consume gluten, there are some who are intolerant to it. being gluten intolerant is different to coeliac disease in that it does not have the same serious health impact. Those who are gluten intolerant will experience symptoms such as bloating, gas and other digestive issues. Other symptoms of gluten intolerance could be headaches, faigue and nausea.
